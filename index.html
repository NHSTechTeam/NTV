What is NTV?

NTV is Newtown CT's Educational access television station. It exists on channel 194 in Newtown CT for Charter customers. The channel is unique in that it is completly student run from Newtown High School.

Next

NTV in the Community

NTV is available for services all over Newtown and can be great for filming all kinds of events, for example, but not limited to:

  • Sporting Events

  • Musical Events

  • Special Occasions

  • Performaces

Next

Want our services?

NTV is available to film any event it may be requested at. Depending on the event and distance from the High School, price of service may vary. However the channel itself is always free. Contact us for more information.
